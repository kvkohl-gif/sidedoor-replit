Task: Restyle “Start a new search / Contacts / Outreach” cards to match UXpilot compact design
Goal: Replace large, padded cards with tighter 3‑up cards; move CTA inside left card; use thin borders, small headings, compact metrics bars.

Acceptance Criteria
- Grid: 3 columns on ≥1024px (`lg:grid-cols-3`), 1 column on mobile, consistent gap `gap-4`.
- Card chrome: `rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-white shadow-sm` with inner padding `p-4` (NOT dotted border). No oversized hero typography.
- Headings: `text-sm font-medium text-gray-700` (e.g., “Start a new search”, “Contacts”, “Outreach”).
- Body copy: `text-xs text-gray-500` for helper lines (“Paste a job description…” / “contacts found” / “messages created”).
- Metrics:
  - Large metric number: `text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-900` (drop from 58/107 hero size).
  - Submetric label inline: `text-xs text-gray-500 ml-2` (e.g., “contacts found”, “messages created”).
  - Bars: 6px height, rounded, neutral track `bg-gray-100`, fill uses:
      - Email verification: `bg-emerald-500`
      - Personalization score: `bg-blue-600`
- Badges:
  - Trend badge (Contacts): small chip `text-[11px] text-emerald-700 bg-emerald-50 border border-emerald-200 px-1.5 py-0.5 rounded` aligned top‑right.
  - “New” badge (Outreach): `text-[11px] text-gray-700 bg-gray-100 px-1.5 py-0.5 rounded` aligned top‑right.
- CTA:
  - Move button **inside** left card, full width on mobile, fixed 220px on desktop.
  - Style: `bg-blue-600 hover:bg-blue-700 text-white rounded-md h-9 text-sm inline-flex items-center justify-center gap-2`.
  - Label: “Find Contacts”.
- Remove the giant top dashed box and oversized H2. Keep the section heading outside the grid if needed.
- A11y: All cards `role="region"` with `aria-labelledby` of the card header; progress bars have `role="progressbar"` with `aria-valuenow`.
