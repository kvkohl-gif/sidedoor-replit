Offer Two Buckets of Outreach Targets
When analyzing a job description or link, you should always aim to generate two sets of contacts:

1. Recruiter Outreach
Based on exact titles like:

Recruiter, Technical Recruiter, Talent Acquisition, People Ops

These are your default/priority for outreach if present in the JD.

Use the parsed name from the job post if available — as you mentioned earlier — and validate via Apollo (or suggest based on email format).

2. Department Lead Outreach
Based on the actual job title, infer one primary department to query in Apollo (no guesses or hallucinations).

From there, source decision-makers like:

“Head of X”, “VP of X”, “Director of X”, or senior ICs

🧠 How to Choose the Right Department (Programmatically)
Here’s a simple deterministic logic tree you can use:

Job Title Includes…	Department to Use in Apollo
“Product Manager”	Product Management
“Software Engineer”	Engineering or Technology
“UX/UI Designer”, “Design”	Design
“Marketing Manager”	Marketing
“Customer Support”, “CX”	Customer Experience
“Operations”, “Chief of Staff”	Operations
“Data Scientist”, “ML”	Data
“Sales”, “Account Executive”	Sales
“HR”, “People”	Human Resources
“Finance”, “Controller”	Finance

Only pass 1 exact match to Apollo. You can still list 2–3 likely departments in the UI, but your API query should be strict and aligned.

🧩 Example UX: Output Structure
Recommended Contacts

🎯 Recruiter Contact:
Christopher Graham — Lead Recruiter
[Verified Email] [Generate Email]

👤 Product Department Lead:
Sarah Patel — Director of Product Management
[Suggested Email] [Generate Email]

This creates credibility + optionality. Users can choose to reach out to both depending on what they’re optimizing for (recruiter pipeline vs decision-maker influence).

⚙️ Implementation Flow
Parse Job Title

Use OpenAI or regex to extract the title from the JD or LinkedIn snippet.

Map to Department

Match title to department using lookup table like above (stored in code or DB).

Apollo Query (x2)

Recruiter: pull based on strict title filters.

Department Lead: pull based on exact department match.

Display Both on UI

Show them in separate sections with distinct icons or headers.

Option to generate custom email for each.
